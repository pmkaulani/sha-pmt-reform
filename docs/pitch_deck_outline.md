# SHA Algorithmic Reform: The "AGI" Pivot
**Presentation Outline for Cabinet Secretaries & Parliamentary Health Committee**

## Slide 1: The Crisis We Can No longer Ignore
- **The Hook:** The "Error by Design" documentary (May 2026) proved what our citizens already knew: the current SHIF Proxy Means Test (PMT) algorithm is fundamentally broken.
- **The Human Cost:** Critically ill patients denied cancer treatment and dialysis because an algorithm overpredicted their income based on their roof material.
- **The Financial Cost:** The KSh 11 Billion fraud scandal (Oct '24 - Apr '25). The system is bleeding money through ghost patients and hidden assets.

## Slide 2: The Root Cause: "Lasso PMT"
- **The Flaw:** The current system uses visual proxies (stone walls, tile roofs) to guess wealth.
- **The Statistics:** 
  - Rural classification accuracy (R²) is only 0.46. 
  - 80% of the poorest households are overcharged.
  - Female-headed households face a 65% exclusion error.
- **The Conclusion:** We are taxing the poor for living in inherited ancestral homes while the wealthy hide their income.

## Slide 3: The Global Standard (What We Missed)
- **The Defense:** SHA cited Colombia (Sisben) and Indonesia (DTKS) to defend the PMT.
- **The Reality:** 
  - Colombia uses continuous retraining and 3 sub-modules. We don't.
  - Indonesia uses community-level verification. We don't.
- **The Pivot:** We must upgrade from a static PMT to a dynamic verification system.

## Slide 4: The Solution: Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) Model v2.1
- **The Shift:** Move away from guessing wealth based on houses, and start *verifying* wealth based on actual data.
- **The Engine:** An ensemble algorithm that mathematically ensures fairness (Equalized Odds difference < 0.05).
- **The Rule:** If you are truly poor, you pay the subsidized rate. If you have hidden wealth, the system finds it.

## Slide 5: The "Triangulation Trinity" (How It Works)
- We cross-reference self-declared data against three hard data sources:
  1. **KRA (Income):** Checks for PINs and declared tax brackets.
  2. **NTSA (Assets):** Checks for vehicle registrations.
  3. **Telcos/M-Pesa (Liquidity):** Checks gross velocity and average retained balance.
- **Result:** We stop guessing. We verify.

## Slide 6: Protecting the Vulnerable (7 Deductions)
- The AGI model is the first in Africa to apply algorithmic compassion:
  1. **Digital Debt Relief:** Fuliza defaults reduce your taxable base.
  2. **Chronic Health Exemption (CHE):** Deductions for long-term illness.
  3. **Tools of Trade:** Bodabodas are depreciated assets, not luxury wealth signals.
  4. **Fiduciary Exemption:** Chama treasurers are not penalized for holding group funds.

## Slide 7: Fraud Engine & Systemic Defense
- The AGI includes a **12-flag fraud detection engine**.
- It instantly flags:
  - Phantom dependents (Household Size vs. Age mismatch)
  - Asset-Liquidity Paradox (No income but high M-Pesa velocity)
  - Geographic Impossibility (Claiming rural poverty while transacting exclusively in Nairobi)
- **Impact:** Stops the bleeding that caused the KSh 11B scandal.

- **Result:** Instant provisional re-tiering in under 120 seconds on a 2G network. Fully compliant with Data Protection Act Section 35 (Right to Human/System Intervention).
